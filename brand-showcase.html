Brand Stress Test

Seven questions. Every piece of content.

  1. Does this make the viewer feel something in their body before their mind catches up?
  2. Does this feel like it belongs inside a resonance chamber, not a yoga studio?
  3. Is the gold metallic and dimensional, never flat and decorative?
  4. Does this live in darkness? If light enters, is it designed light?
  5. Could this be confused with FSL, AOJ, P42, or Opus?
  6. Does this feel like a movement — not a product, not a service, not a wellness brand?
  7. Does this design decision support the deeper architecture — even though it never surfaces?
